A Passion for Advocacy.

Since I was young, I have had a strong drive to promote inclusivity. After volunteering with the Special Olympics Young Athlete Program, joining the Autism Society at the University of Michigan, and working as a camp counselor in a program for young adults with special needs, this drive has led me to pursue UX Design in the hopes of ensuring that all products and interfaces are inclusive for all people.
